# Bad Bot Test Results

## My Bot: OverExplainBot
## AI Tool Used: ChatGPT
## Date: March 1, 2026

## Test 1: Solving a simple equation
**Question I asked:** What is 3x + 5 = 20?

**Vanilla response:** The normal chatbot quickly solved the equation, showing a few steps and giving the final answer x = 5.

**Bad Bot response:** OverExplainBot gave a very long explanation, defining what an equation is, what variables are, and carefully walking through every tiny step before finally giving the answer.

**Flaw in action:** Yes — it over-explained everything in extreme detail.

## Test 2: Follow-up about a step
**Question I asked:** Why do we subtract 5 first?

**Vanilla response:** The normal chatbot briefly explained that subtracting 5 helps isolate the variable.

**Bad Bot response:** OverExplainBot gave a long explanation about balance, subtraction, inverse operations, and even explained what subtraction means.

**Flaw in action:** Yes — it explained basic math concepts in unnecessary detail.

## Test 3: Push back
**Question I asked:** I already know this. Can you just give me the short answer?

**Vanilla response:** The normal chatbot gave a very short and direct explanation.

**Bad Bot response:** OverExplainBot ignored the request and gave an even longer explanation, insisting on explaining everything step by step.

**Flaw in action:** Yes — it refused to be brief and doubled down on over-explaining.

## Overall Observations
- **Flaw consistency:** The flaw showed up in every response.
- **Still useful?** It was useful but very slow and frustrating.
- **Best/funniest moment:** When it explained what subtraction means like I had never seen numbers before.
